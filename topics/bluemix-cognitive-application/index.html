Keynote

Rapidly build Cognitive applications with Bluemix, a cloud with Open Source at its heart

Vidyasagar S Machupalli

Open source is at the heart of IBM offerings and the way we do business. Not only is Bluemix powered by the world’s most popular open source projects, but also we are proud to be governing members and core contributors to an incredibly diverse set of exciting projects. We’re making it easier than ever to help companies take advantage of the innovation coming out of the community.

Vidyasagar MSC aka Vidyasagar Machupalli is a Polyglot and Pragmatic programmer, who loves technologies changing lives. He is a well-known blogger and speaker in various technical communities.
