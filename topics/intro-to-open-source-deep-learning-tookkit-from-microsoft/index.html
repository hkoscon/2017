Intro to Open Source Deep Learning toolkit from Microsoft

Day 1 (9th June) 02:30 PM - 03:00 PM
Function Room 1 and 2
English (English Slides)
For Coders and Tech Audiences with General Knowledge and Skills.
Get your ticket

Speaker

Delon Yau

Technical Evangelist, DX, Microsoft

Delon is currently a Technical Evangelist in the Developer Experience Group at Microsoft HK, evangelizing developers across HK on artificial intelligence on cloud, big data analytics and IoT. Prior to Joining Microsoft HK, he worked for Microsoft UK as a machine learning developer supporting the data analytics development of a cloud-based online shopping platform.

He holds a Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Imperial College London and also a Master's degree in Computer Science from UCL.

Nationality: Hong Kong
